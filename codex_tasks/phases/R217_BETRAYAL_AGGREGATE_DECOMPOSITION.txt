DECOMPOSITION RULES:
A decomposed row is ready only if:
- timeframe is known
- original_direction is explicit
- inverse_direction is explicit and opposite original_direction
- entry_mode is explicit
- source_identity or source_signal_id exists
- signal_timestamp exists
- paper_only true
- live_authorized false

A row is partial if:
- original/inverse direction exists but entry_mode or source identity is missing

A row is blocked if:
- aggregate-only with no explicit direction
- direction inferred only from lane key
- no timestamp
- no identity

Do not infer entry_mode from default ladder mode unless source explicitly identifies it.
Do not infer source_identity from candidate label alone.
