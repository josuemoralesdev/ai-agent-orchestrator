DIRECTION COMPLETION RULES:
A direction field may be completed only if:
- original_direction exists explicitly in source row; OR
- source/original direction is present in shadow outcome / direction split row; OR
- source signal id explicitly contains direction; OR
- lane_key explicitly contains direction AND the row source clearly identifies whether lane direction is original or emitted direction.

inverse_direction may be completed only if:
- original_direction exists; and
- inverse_direction is explicitly present; OR
- inverse_direction can be safely defined as the opposite of original_direction under a betrayal/inverse source-family row.

emitted_direction may be completed only if:
- inverse_direction exists; and
- source family is betrayal/inverse; and
- emitted_direction equals inverse_direction.

- infer direction from aggregate label

- treat lane direction as inverse_direction unless source family proves it
