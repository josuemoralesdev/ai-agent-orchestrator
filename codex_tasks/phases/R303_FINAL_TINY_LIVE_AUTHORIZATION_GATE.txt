Purpose:
  Add the final tiny-live authorization gate that can certify the one-shot live readiness boundary.
  This gate must remain WAIT/BLOCKED until a real matching fresh live-qualified candidate exists.
  When all conditions are true, it may produce a final manual operator submit command packet.
  It must never execute the command itself.

    - final_command_available=false
    - submit_allowed=false
    - real_order_forbidden=true
