AUTHORIZATION CONCEPT:
A lane may be authorized for future tiny-live autonomous operation only if:

1. Lane exists.
2. Lane is eligible for future tiny-live or explicitly allowed by config/risk contract.
3. Lane has recent paper proof from R125/R129 or a recent autonomous paper integration record.
4. R126 tiny-live gate has been evaluated for that lane.
5. Risk contract exists.
6. Max daily trades/loss/cooldown are present.
7. Protective order policy is required.
8. Emergency rollback path is acknowledged.
9. Kill switch semantics are acknowledged.
10. Operator supplies exact tiny-live authorization confirmation phrase.
11. Safety fields remain false.
12. No Binance/order/network/secret behavior occurs.

R130 may still return AUTHORIZATION_BLOCKED if prerequisites are missing.
