Why this phase exists:
  The operator wants to test a distinct ultra-short high-leverage paper strategy for very short 4m and 8m signals.

  Hypothesis:
    - Open immediately when a valid 4m or 8m signal appears.
    - Use very short holding windows and checkpoint loops.
    - Test leverage grid: 22x, 44x, 88x, 150x.
    - Test exit checkpoint grid: 22s, 44s, 66s, 88s, 132s, 176s.
    - Target fast ROE capture, especially around 150x.
    - Evaluate net-after-fees and slippage, not gross fantasy ROE.

D. Signal source policy:
  Define:
    - signal must come from existing radar/strategy signal surfaces
    - entry timestamp must be exact signal detection time if available
    - if only candle close is available, mark fill model as approximate
    - no stale signal replay for live-like evidence
    - signal age must be measured
    - immediate entry means first valid paper fill after detection timestamp, not next candle close unless that is the only available proxy

E. Leverage grid:
  Include:
    - 22x
    - 44x
    - 88x
    - 150x

  For each leverage:
    - gross ROE target examples
    - approximate price move required for 15% gross ROE
    - liquidation proximity warning
    - fee drag sensitivity
    - live_permission=false

  Required approximate formula:
    price_move_pct_for_gross_roe = gross_roe_pct / leverage

  Example:
    15% gross ROE at 150x requires about 0.10% favorable price move before fees/slippage.

F. Checkpoint grid:
  Include:
    - 22 seconds
    - 44 seconds
    - 66 seconds
    - 88 seconds
    - 132 seconds
    - 176 seconds

  Include:
    - first 44s checkpoint as primary operator concept
    - 66s and 88s as second-wave checks
    - 22s increments as optional micro-checks
    - hard timeout exit design
    - early take profit design
    - adverse velocity exit design

G. Exit policy design:
  Include:
    - gross_roe_target_grid:
        5%, 10%, 15%, 22%
    - minimum_net_roe_target_grid:
        2%, 3%, 5%, 8%
    - hard_loss_roe_grid:
        -5%, -8%, -10%, -12%
    - timeout_seconds_grid:
        44, 66, 88, 132, 176
    - trailing-after-target design
    - close at first net target design
    - close at hard timeout design
    - close on adverse velocity design
    - no averaging down
    - no second entry unless new independent signal
    - no gross-only success classification

H. Fee/slippage model design:
  Include:
    - gross_roe is insufficient
    - net_roe_after_fees required
    - fee model must account for round-trip maker/taker assumptions
    - slippage model must account for entry and exit
    - latency model must be represented separately
    - unknown fees/slippage must make result lower-confidence
    - never mark a burst candidate ready using gross ROE only

  Include formula fields:
    notional_fee_pct_round_trip
    fee_drag_roe = notional_fee_pct_round_trip * leverage
    estimated_slippage_roe = estimated_slippage_pct_round_trip * leverage
    net_roe = gross_roe - fee_drag_roe - estimated_slippage_roe

I. Liquidation proximity design:
  Include:
    - estimated adverse price move to danger zone
    - liquidation proximity should be modeled, not ignored
    - 150x requires explicit liquidation warning
    - position sizing must be microscopic in any future tiny-burst-live
    - isolated margin only in any future preview
    - no cross margin
    - no live permission in R333A/R333B/R333C/R333D

J. Required market data design:
  Include:
    - best: tick/trade data or second-level mark/last price
    - acceptable for early lab: sub-minute replay if available
    - poor: candle-only OHLC
    - candle-only OHLC cannot prove whether TP or SL happened first
    - candle-only OHLC must be marked sequence_unknown
    - sequence_unknown rows cannot promote to live
    - capture requirements for future R333B

K. Evidence contract:
  For any future promotion from burst paper lab to any live preview, require:
    - sample_count >= 100 minimum per side/timeframe/leverage/exit profile
    - preferred sample_count >= 300
    - net win rate after fees/slippage >= 60%
    - profit factor > 1.3
    - max adverse excursion known
    - timeout behavior known
    - fee model included
    - slippage model included
    - latency model included
    - liquidation proximity model included
    - sequence_known=true preferred
    - no gross-only ROE readiness
    - no candle-only fantasy fills
    - no stale shadow outcomes
    - no standard 55% policy
    - no Tiny Live inheritance
    - separate human-reviewed burst risk contract required
