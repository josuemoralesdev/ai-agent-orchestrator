     If a fresh candidate exists on approved live-qualified lane but risk contract missing/invalid:
       status = ONE_SHOT_PRE_ACTIVATION_BLOCKED

     If all read-only gates green and fresh approved candidate exists:
       status = ONE_SHOT_PRE_ACTIVATION_READY_FOR_DRY_RUN_TRIGGER
       final command still unavailable in R285.
       next_required_step = RUN_ONE_SHOT_DRY_RUN_TRIGGER_PACKET_OR_ARMING_REVIEW
       no live order.
