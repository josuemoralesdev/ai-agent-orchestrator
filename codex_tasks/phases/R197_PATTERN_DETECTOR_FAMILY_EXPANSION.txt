PATTERN DEFINITIONS:

1. three_white_soldiers
- direction: long
- bullish mirror of Three Black Crows
- requires 3 consecutive bullish candles
- each candle close > open
- closes step upward:
  close_3 > close_2 > close_1
- strict mode body ratio >= 0.5
- loose_preview body ratio >= 0.35
- opens ideally within/near previous real body, soft in preview

2. bearish_engulfing
- direction: short
- 2-candle pattern
- previous candle bullish or neutral
- current candle bearish
- current real body engulfs previous real body
- close < previous open and open > previous close in strict mode
- loose_preview may allow close below previous midpoint and body dominance

3. bullish_engulfing
- direction: long
- 2-candle pattern
- previous candle bearish or neutral
- current candle bullish
- current real body engulfs previous real body
- close > previous open and open < previous close in strict mode
- loose_preview may allow close above previous midpoint and body dominance

4. exhaustion_wick
- direction: long/short
- long setup: lower wick exhaustion
  lower_wick_ratio high, close recovers above midpoint
- short setup: upper wick exhaustion
  upper_wick_ratio high, close rejects below midpoint
- strict threshold wick_ratio >= 0.6
- loose_preview wick_ratio >= 0.45

5. breakdown_retest
- direction: short
- registry/preview only unless existing local structure supports swing/retest detection
- do not fake detections

6. breakout_retest
- direction: long
- registry/preview only unless existing local structure supports swing/retest detection
- do not fake detections
