PHASE:
R122 Autonomous Lane Control Scaffold

BRANCH:
r122-autonomous-lane-control-scaffold

PHASE CLASSIFICATION:
Primary: NEW CAPABILITY
Secondary: WIRING / INTEGRATION
Duplicate risk: MEDIUM

GOAL:
Create a non-executing per-lane control scaffold so the operator can arm/disarm strategy lanes instead of approving stale individual signals.

A lane is:
symbol + timeframe + direction + entry_mode

Example:
BTCUSDT | 13m | long | ladder_close_50_618

WHY:
Manual approval of individual 4m/8m/13m signals is structurally too slow. The operator should arm a lane ahead of time, then the system waits for fresh matching signals under strict risk controls.
