CONFLUENCE RESOLUTION:
- event_level:
  Exact timestamps/candles are matched between anchor event and signal origin detection.
- summary_level:
  Timeframe/source overlap exists, but exact candle timestamps are not matched.
- none:
  No useful overlap.

Scoring must penalize summary_level vs event_level.

SCORING RULES:
For each confluence row:
Inputs:
- signal_origin_score:
  from R205 pair score or R204 Keter score
- anchor_score:
  from R201 anchor interaction ranking
- lane_score:
  from R205 lane matrix if available
- sample_confidence:
  high/medium/low from R201 and pattern evidence
- confluence_resolution:
  event_level > summary_level > none
- direction_alignment:
  signal direction should align with anchor interaction bias
- risk warnings:
  penalize high failure rate / adverse > favorable / mixed bias
- configured lane:
  configured lanes get less caution than discovered unconfigured lanes
- fresh flow:
  fresh > stale_only > unknown

Suggested score:
- 30% signal origin / pair score
- 25% anchor score
- 20% lane evidence / flow
- 15% sample confidence / mapped events
- 10% direction alignment
Penalties:
- summary_level_only confluence
- high risk warnings
- stale-only flow
- discovered unconfigured lane
- blocked origins
- no event-level timestamp match

Never convert score into live permission.
