Purpose:
  Add a certificate proving that, after manual operator dry-run arming, the running timer/scheduler observes the exact armed lane state and remains safely in WAIT until a real matching fresh candidate appears.

Hard goal:
  When exact lane is armed and no real matching fresh candidate exists:
    - status=ARMED_DRY_RUN_TIMER_OBSERVATION_WAIT_CERTIFIED
    - exact_lane_auto_armed=true
    - armed_lane_key=requested_lane_key
    - timer_active=true
    - recent_tick_seen=true
    - scheduler_latest_status observed
    - scheduler/latest trigger loop remains WAIT
    - no order/submit/payload

  If a real matching candidate appears later:
    - status may become ARMED_DRY_RUN_TIMER_OBSERVATION_TRIGGER_READY_CERTIFIED or equivalent
    - still dry-run only
    - still no final live command
    - still no order/submit/payload
