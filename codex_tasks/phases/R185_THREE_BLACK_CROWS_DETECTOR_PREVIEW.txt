PATTERN DEFINITION:
Three Black Crows bearish detector should initially require:
- exactly 3 consecutive bearish candles
- each candle close < open
- candle closes step downward:
  close_3 < close_2 < close_1
- candle opens ideally within or near previous candle body, but preview may allow soft mode
- bodies should be meaningful relative to candle range
- upper/lower wick filters optional in preview
- pattern direction = short
- signal_origin = three_black_crows
- detector_version = r185_preview

Support two modes:
1. strict:
   - three bearish candles
   - consecutive lower closes
   - opens within previous real body or near it
   - body ratio threshold >= 0.5
2. loose_preview:
   - three bearish candles
   - consecutive lower closes
   - body ratio threshold >= 0.35

If local OHLC data is unavailable:
- status should be THREE_BLACK_CROWS_DETECTOR_BLOCKED
- blocker: missing_ohlc_feed
- recommend future candle feed integration
- do not fake detections
