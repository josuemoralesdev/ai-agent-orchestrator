- registry candidate has exactly one allowed entry mode AND source explicitly references that mode.

A normalized row may fill source_identity only if:
- source_identity exists; OR
- source_signal_id exists; OR

- emitted_signal_id exists; OR
- deterministic source identity can be built from symbol/timeframe/direction/entry_mode/timestamp/source family.

A normalized row may fill emitted_direction only if:
- inverse_direction exists; and
- emitted_direction can equal inverse_direction.
