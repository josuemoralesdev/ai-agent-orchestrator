OUTCOME WINDOWS:
For each detection, compute post-detection outcome over:
- 1 candle
- 3 candles
- 5 candles
- 10 candles

For short context:
- entry_reference = close of detection candle or next candle open if available
- mfe_downside_pct = best downside move after detection
- mae_upside_pct = worst upside move against detection
- close_return_pct = (future_close - entry_reference) / entry_reference * 100
  For short, negative close_return_pct is favorable.
- favorable_close = close_return_pct < 0
- adverse_close = close_return_pct > 0
- simple_success = mfe_downside_pct >= configurable threshold, default 0.10%
- simple_failure = mae_upside_pct >= configurable adverse threshold, default 0.10%
